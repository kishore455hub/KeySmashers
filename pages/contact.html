For Game Developers 🎮

At KeySmashers 💥, we believe typing practice should be exciting and fun. That’s why we welcome game developers to share their creations!

If you have developed a keyboard typing game, you can upload it to our platform so players around the world can enjoy and improve their skills while playing your game. 🚀

Simply reach out to us with your project details, and we’ll guide you through the process of adding your game to KeySmashers.
